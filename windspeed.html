Wind Speed


Per the data, there appears to be no relationship between latitude and wind speed. However, it is important to note that most of the data is concentrated where the wind speed is less than 15 mph with several outliers going beyond 20 mph. Most of these outliers are in the northern hemiphere. I believe this makes sense, given that per the previous page on temperature, the more severe low temperatures occurred in northern hemisphere that day (between 60 to 80 degrees N). The greater the temperature differential, the greater the potential difference in air pressure, so there should be areas where there are more severe winds. However, this only applies to the outliers, and this is not endemic of the dataset as a whole.
